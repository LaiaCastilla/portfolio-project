I'm Laia

A junior front-end developer based in Swizerland

Originally from Barcelona, I have always been an unusually imaginative and curious person. Being the latter the reason why I moved to Switzerland to pursue a PhD in Chemistry. It was then when I started to explore the digital media as means of creation, being always extremely enthusiastic whenever I had to use Adobe Illustrator to create images that could visually explain in an intuitive way the scientific advances we were doing.

During a needed break following my studies, I realised how much I had craved creativity during the last few years. I then discovered the art of crafting aesthetically pleasing and accessible webpages, visual essays and storytelling. I was amazed by the work of numerous front-end developers and webdesigners. I would love to do that! I told myself. What is stoping you? I answered. Not knowing how to do it being the only reasonable answer I could give, I decided to take a leap and do with my life whatever makes me happy.

I am now forming myself as a front-end developer, feeling at ease with the coding logics and the idea of constantly changing languages! I am looking forward to growing as a developer, learning from my peers and slowly but surely becoming able of creating webpages as inspiring as the ones that got me here!
